Why CaseFlow

Built differently from every other tool you've tried

Accounting software stores invoices. Chaser chases payments. CaseFlow does something neither can: it organises the evidence when a dispute happens, so you arrive at the negotiation with facts, not frustration.

Evidence-first, not reminder-first

Every dispute gets a structured evidence map linking your invoice to the PO, delivery note, and email trail. Gaps are flagged automatically, not discovered in a last-minute email search.

Legal-safe by design

CaseFlow produces factual summaries, not legal opinions. Templates are built with built-in disclaimers so your finance team can create professional documentation without accidentally giving legal advice.

Prevents future disputes too

Once a case is closed, CaseFlow identifies the root cause and suggests an internal control like requiring delivery proof before invoicing to stop the same dispute recurring next month.

No ERP, no IT department needed

CaseFlow starts with CSV import and file uploads no complex accounting integration required. A finance manager can be up and running with a real dispute in under 20 minutes.

One structured "version of events"

The platform builds a single, chronological view of what happened contract → order → delivery → invoice → dispute eliminating the back-and-forth about who said what and when.

SME-priced, not enterprise-licensed

Enterprise AR tools cost £10,000–£100,000 per year and require dedicated IT teams. CaseFlow is priced at £59–299/month designed for businesses with 10 to 80 employees.
